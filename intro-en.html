Signature Feature

A missed word doesn't just get crossed off —
it follows the student until it's truly learned

Whether a word is missed in Match, Practice, or a Live Game round, it's automatically added to that student's own My Mistakes list. From there, students can pull up just their missed words for a focused round of Match or Practice.

A word doesn't disappear the first time it's answered right — it takes 3 correct answers, cumulative (a wrong answer in between doesn't reset the count), before it's actually cleared. That way a lucky guess never gets mistaken for real mastery.

How it works
01 Miss a word in Practice or a Live Game → it's added to My Mistakes
02 Students can select My Mistakes as a pool for Flashcards / Match / Practice
03 After 3 cumulative correct answers, the word is cleared automatically
